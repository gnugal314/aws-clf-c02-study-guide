# AWS CLF-C02 14-Day Study Plan

## Daily Study Format

Each day should include:

1. Study the concept.
2. Review keyword triggers.
3. Complete at least 10 practice questions.
4. Complete scenario-based questions.
5. Update your service mapping sheet.

---

## Day 1 — Cloud Fundamentals

### Topics
- Cloud computing
- Pay-as-you-go pricing
- OpEx vs CapEx
- Elasticity
- Scalability
- Agility

### Practice Focus
Map business value to cloud adoption benefits.

---
